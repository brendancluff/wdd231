Site Name

Dragon Rider Academy

This name clearly communicates the theme and purpose: a training academy for riders to learn dragon care, flight basics, safety, and team bonding. It is memorable, direct, and fits a fantasy academy brand.

Optional domain idea: dragonrideracademy.org (availability not required for class)

Site Purpose

The Dragon Rider Academy website serves as a hub for future riders to explore programs, meet dragon breeds, review training schedules, and apply for enrollment. The site will include a dynamically generated directory of dragons and/or courses using JSON + Fetch, support user preferences with localStorage (such as favorite dragons or preferred training track), and offer an application/contact form.

  • Learn about the academy, tracks, rules, and safety standards.
  • Browse a directory of dragons and/or training courses with filters.
  • Open a modal dialog to view detailed dragon or course information.
  • Save favorites or settings (localStorage) to persist preferences.
  • Submit an enrollment inquiry form and view the submitted data on an action page.

Scenarios

These are questions a typical visitor might ask:

  • “Which dragon breed is best for beginner riders and calmer flight training?”
  • “What training tracks does the academy offer, and what are the prerequisites?”
  • “Can I save a shortlist of dragons or courses I’m interested in so I can compare later?”

Color Scheme

Based on standard color palette guidance, we selected a dominant primary color and two complementary accent colors, plus a planned background and text color. Each color has a specific role to support accessibility, readability, and brand recognition.

Primary: #0F172A (Dragon Night Blue)

Main color used for headings, header background, and strong UI emphasis.

Accent 1: #F97316 (Ember Orange)

Used for buttons, links, and text highlights to attract attention and contrast with primary color.

Accent 2: #4E9F3D (Emerald Green)

Used sparingly for secondary UI elements and contrast (e.g., success states or badges).

Background: #F8FAFC (Cloud Mist)

Used for the body background to support readability and avoid eye strain.

Text: #24292E (Dark Charcoal)

Used for main body text; softer than pure black for more comfortable reading on screens.

Typography

Based on best practices for web fonts, we selected fonts that combine readability with fantasy-style personality. These fonts come from Google Fonts, ensuring wide support and consistent rendering.

  • Heading font: Cormorant Garamond — elegant and thematic, used for H1/H2 elements to give a classic “academy” feel.
  • Body font: Inter — highly readable sans-serif used for paragraphs, captions, and UI text.

Planned Pages (3 total)

  1. Home (index.html): hero, academy overview, highlights, and entry points.
  2. Dragons (dragons.html): fetch JSON to display 15+ dragons (or courses), show 4+ properties per item, filter/search, modal details, save favorites (localStorage).

Extra pages (not counted toward the 3): form action page (e.g., form-response.html) and an attributions page (if external content is used).
